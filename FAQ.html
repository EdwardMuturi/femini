FAQ

  1. FOR HOW LONG HAS FEMINI BEEN ACTIVE AS AN INVETSMENT PLATFORM?

  3. HOW FEMINI GENERATES PROFIT FOR PARTICIPATING PRIVATE INVESTOR?
    Femini is currently engaged in trading on Forex and Stock markets, capitalizing mostly on short term, technical analysis based positions and on occasion on longer term, global events driven long term profitable projections. In all cases we are utilizing our proprietary unique technology and in house developed trading algorithms.

  4. ARE THE INVESTED FUNDS INSURED AND OFFERED RETURNS GUARANTEED?
    Femini's technology has demonstrated exceptionally consistent long term profits. However, we are proud to insure invested funds and to offer peace of mind to the investors.

  5. HOW SECURE IS THE DATA SUCH AS NAME AND AMOUNT INVESTED?
    Security and complete privacy of clients' data are among the main objectives of our program. We would never pass or disclose any private information to any third parties, except when requested by law enforcement agents. We took every possible technological precaution to protect clients' accounts data from possible breaches.

  6. HOW CAN USERS CONTACT FEMINI INCASE THEY HAVE ACCOUNT OR RALATED QUESTION?

  7. WHO CAN PARTICIPATE IN THE INVESTMENT PLATFORM AND CAN I DO WITHOUT OPENING AN ACCOUNT ?
    Program participation is open to any individual 18 years or older, with Internet access, who has accepted Femini terms of service. In order to participate in our program, everyone is required to create the account. We fully rely on user accounts data when planning and estimating required by the program fund transfers.

  8. CAN I MAKE ADDITIONAL DEPOSIT AND OR CAN I MANAGE TWO OR MORE ACCOUNTS?
